About Raspberry Ice Cream

Raspberry ice cream is a delicious and refreshing treat made with fresh raspberries and a creamy ice cream base. Perfect for a hot summer day, it combines the tartness of raspberries with the sweetness of cream, creating an irresistible flavor.

Interesting Facts

  • Raspberries are rich in vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants.
  • The vibrant color of Raspberry ice cream is all-natural.
  • Combining dairy with raspberries can help improve calcium absorption.

Recipe

  1. In a blender, puree the raspberries until smooth.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar, heavy cream, and whole milk. Cook over medium heat until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the pureed raspberries and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Transfer the ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.
